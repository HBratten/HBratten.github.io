About Me

Hi! I'm Hillary. I am a Front-End Web Developer with a background in event management. Much like event planning, I enjoy front-end web development because I love creating an experience that people can interact with and enjoy.

Born and raised in Rochester, New York, but currently located in Detroit, Michigan. I graduated from Allegheny College with my Bachelor of Arts, with a focus on Communication Arts. After working in the event world for five years, I decided I wanted a career where I could do problem solving and learn something new everyday. That decision led me to where I am today as a web developer.

Some things you should know about me: I am obsessed with Wegmans grocery stores, I love Taylor Swift, the holiday season is my favorite time of year, and I am always smiling!
